# swap-app

An NextJS SSR app which helps consumers make balnace consumer product purchases between sustainability, affordability, product quality and health and wellbeing’s. Once user set his preferences, he can scan any product, and we would show them the SWAPPED product which would better align on their goals, scoring higher than the product originally selected. EG:- I bought a dove bar soap, and the app swapped it for a dove body refillable pouch, which has better sustainability, wellbeing’s, and quality scores than the soap bar, as per my preferences.
